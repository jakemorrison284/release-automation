# Feedback Process for Automated Versioning

## Purpose
To gather insights and improve the automated versioning process through regular communication and feedback from the development and QA teams.

## Feedback Sessions
- **Frequency**: Monthly feedback sessions will be held.
- **Participants**: Development team, QA team, and relevant stakeholders.
- **Agenda**:
  - Review the effectiveness of the automated versioning system.
  - Discuss any issues or concerns encountered during the release process.
  - Identify potential improvements to the versioning logic and monitoring.

## Documentation of Feedback
- All feedback and action items will be documented in a shared document.
- Responsible parties will be assigned for follow-up actions.

## Communication Channels
- Use channels like Slack or email for ongoing feedback and quick discussions.
- Encourage team members to share insights or raise concerns as they arise.

## Review and Adjust
- The feedback process will be reviewed quarterly to ensure it meets team needs and can be adjusted as necessary.
